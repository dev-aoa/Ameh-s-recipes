Banana Bread Recipe

Description

Banana bread is a type of sweet bread that is made using mashed ripe bananas as the main ingredient. It is a popular baked good in many countries, and its origins can be traced back to the Great Depression when households needed to make the most of their ingredients. Banana bread is typically made with a combination of flour, sugar, eggs, butter or oil, and baking powder or soda. Other ingredients such as nuts, chocolate chips, or cinnamon can also be added to enhance the flavor. Banana bread is often served as a breakfast or dessert item and is enjoyed by many for its moist texture and delicious banana flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instrucctions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork.
  3. Add the melted butter to the mashed bananas and mix well.
  4. Add baking soda and salt, and stir.
  5. Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  6. Add the flour and mix until just combined.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the loaf comes out clean.
  9. Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove it from the pan and cool on a wire rack.
  10. Slice and serve. Enjoy your delicious banana bread!
